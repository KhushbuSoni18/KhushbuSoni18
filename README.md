### 🛒 Retail Sales Analysis — Walmart
**Tool:** MySQL &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Dataset:** 10,000+ sales records

Analyzed store performance across product categories and time periods. Used JOINs, CTEs, window functions, and aggregations to identify top-performing products, revenue trends, and underperforming segments.

**Key findings:**
- Identified highest and lowest revenue-generating product lines
- Tracked monthly sales patterns to surface demand trends

**Key findings:**
